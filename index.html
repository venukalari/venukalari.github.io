Welcome to the homepage of Venu Kalari




I am an astronomer at the Gemini Observatory in Chile, supporting science operations. I also spend half of my time at Universidad de Chile, looking at all things stellar. My research uses data from large and small telescopes, to help us understand better how stars form and evolve, and how this influences the way the Universe is.
